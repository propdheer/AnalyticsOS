# Product Glossary

## AnalyticsOS

A local-first Professional Intelligence Platform.

## Professional Memory

Durable knowledge about the user's work, decisions, preferences, projects, and lessons learned.

## Knowledge Asset

Any file or artifact that contains useful professional knowledge.

Examples include Markdown, Excel, PowerPoint, PDF, SQL, Python, Power BI files, and AI conversations.

## Project DNA

The accumulated context of a project, including purpose, datasets, dashboards, decisions, code, outputs, and lessons learned.

## Knowledge Card

A structured representation of an important object such as a project, dataset, business rule, prompt, or decision.

## Capability

A stable product-level ability, such as Knowledge, Memory, Execution, Intelligence, or Experience.

## Plugin

A modular implementation that adds a capability without changing AnalyticsOS Core.

## Human-in-the-Loop

A design principle where AI output is reviewed and approved by a human before important changes or actions occur.
