# AnalyticsOS Manifesto

## Local First

Professional knowledge belongs to the person who creates it.

## Knowledge Compounds

A useful intelligence platform should become more valuable every week.

## Human First

AI should assist, not replace, professional judgment.

## Explainability

Every answer should be traceable to its sources, memories, and reasoning path.

## Plugin First

The platform should evolve through modular capabilities rather than hardcoded features.
