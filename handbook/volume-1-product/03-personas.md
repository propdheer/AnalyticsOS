# Personas

## Persona 1: Data Science Lead

### Context

Leads analytics initiatives across business functions. Works with datasets, dashboards, code, presentations, and stakeholders.

### Needs

- Remember project context
- Track datasets and business rules
- Generate executive summaries
- Search prior analysis
- Reuse prompts and templates
- Automate repeatable workflows

### AnalyticsOS Value

AnalyticsOS becomes a professional memory and execution layer across projects.

## Persona 2: Analyst

### Context

Builds reports, analyzes Excel files, prepares dashboards, and responds to business requests.

### Needs

- Find prior work
- Understand dataset definitions
- Generate analysis notes
- Create repeatable workflows
- Avoid rebuilding the same logic repeatedly

### AnalyticsOS Value

AnalyticsOS helps analysts move faster by preserving reusable knowledge and prior work.

## Persona 3: Consultant

### Context

Works across clients or functions and needs to preserve frameworks, deliverables, and lessons learned.

### Needs

- Reuse prior frameworks
- Generate client-ready outputs
- Maintain a knowledge library
- Track decisions and assumptions

### AnalyticsOS Value

AnalyticsOS becomes a reusable consulting knowledge base.
