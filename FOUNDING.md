## Why AnalyticsOS Exists

AnalyticsOS was created from a simple but important realization:

> Most professionals do not suffer from a lack of information. They suffer because their knowledge does not compound.

A professional learns thousands of valuable things every year: which datasets are trustworthy, which dashboards were built and why, which business rules changed, which SQL logic worked, which prompts produced useful outputs, and which decisions shaped projects.

Most of that knowledge disappears into folders, chats, notebooks, slides, emails, and memory.

AnalyticsOS exists to preserve that knowledge and make it useful again.

## Original Vision

AnalyticsOS began as a local AI second brain for a data science leader working in an Indian FMCG context.

The idea evolved into something more ambitious:

> A local-first professional intelligence platform that understands projects, datasets, decisions, prompts, business rules, and analytical workflows.

## What Should Never Change

1. Users own their knowledge.
2. Local-first must remain a first-class mode.
3. AI must remain explainable.
4. Automation must remain human-controlled by default.
5. Knowledge should be structured, traceable, and reusable.
6. The platform must be modular enough to outlive any single AI model or vendor.

## Founding Motto

> Build Once. Learn Forever.
